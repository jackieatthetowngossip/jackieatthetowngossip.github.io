Saints in Seven Film Productions

Heroes for every generation

Saints in Seven is a non-for profit film production company that seeks to inspire Catholic youth to live out their Confirmation anointing. With full length biographies and short seven minute videos on the lives of the saints, we remind today's youth that devotion to the sacraments and love for Jesus Christ can make the ordinary person rise to extraordinary holiness and change the world around them.

Pier Giorgio Frassati was a modern fun-loving guy who also had a deep devotion to Jesus and serving the poor. Learn how he makes a great model for Catholic youth today.

We inspire heroes for a time such as this

Our children are growing up in a caotic and confusting world. Everyone is connected, yet lonelier than ever. They are bombarded by input, influencers, posts, and texts. What is real? What is illusion? What is life about and is there anything worth living for?

More than ever, kids need real heroes not computer or movie avatars. Uncommon human beings that have discovered the secret to true happiness is worth suffering and sometimes even dying for. Role models for today's generation that witness to the freedom found in the truth of Christ.

Every saint inspires the one to come after him.
